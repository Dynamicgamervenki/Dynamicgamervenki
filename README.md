### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a 2025 Computer Science graduate with specialized experience in game development, tools programming, and animation pipelines. I am passionate about crafting seamless interactive experiences and building the tools that bring characters to life.

- ⚙️ **Engines & Animation:** I work extensively with **Unreal Engine 5** and **Unity**, alongside **MotionBuilder** for animation cleanup, mocap polishing, and character workflows.
- 💻 **Programming & Rendering:** I am comfortable programming in **C++, C#, and Python**, with hands-on experience using **UXML, USS**, alongside **CGSL, HLSL, and OpenGL** for rendering and UI tasks.
- 🚀 **Projects & Publishing:** I successfully self-published my game [**Final Turn**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dynamicV.FinalTurn&pcampaignid=web_share) and maintain an active portfolio showcasing my experiments and learning projects.
- 📺 **Knowledge Sharing:** I create and share **MotionBuilder tutorials** focusing on mocap workflow improvements, animation cleanup, and fixing foot sliding.
